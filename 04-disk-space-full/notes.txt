Commands:
df -h
du -sh /*
du -sh /var/*
find / -type f -size +100M
find /var/log -type f

Investigation Flow:

1. Check filesystem utilization.
2. Identify large directories.
3. Locate large files.
4. Verify if logs, backups, or temporary files are consuming space.
5. Clean up safely.

Learning:
A full filesystem can cause application failures, service outages, and failed deployments.
Never delete files without identifying ownership and purpose.
