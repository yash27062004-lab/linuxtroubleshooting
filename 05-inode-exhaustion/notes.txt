Commands:
df -i
find /var -type f | wc -l
find /tmp -type f | wc -l
find / -xdev -type f

Investigation Flow:

1. Check inode utilization.
2. Identify directories containing large numbers of files.
3. Verify whether logs, cache files, or temporary files are causing inode exhaustion.
4. Remove unnecessary files.
5. Recheck inode usage.

Learning:
Disk space and inodes are separate resources.
A million small files can exhaust inodes while consuming very little disk space.
