Commands:
uptime
top
htop
ps aux --sort=-%cpu | head
top -H -p PID
pgrep

Investigation Flow:

1. Check system load.
2. Identify top CPU-consuming processes.
3. Inspect process threads.
4. Verify whether CPU usage is expected.

Learning:
High load does not always mean high CPU.
Always find the process before killing or restarting anything.
