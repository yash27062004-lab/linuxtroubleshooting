Commands:
ps aux
ps -el
pstree
top
htop

Investigation Flow:

1. Identify zombie processes.
2. Find the parent PID (PPID).
3. Inspect the parent process.
4. Restart or terminate the parent if required.
5. Verify zombie cleanup.

Learning:
A zombie process has already exited.
It remains in the process table until the parent collects its exit status.
