Commands:
ps aux
ps aux --sort=-%cpu
ps aux --sort=-%mem
pgrep
pstree
top
htop
kill
pkill

Investigation Flow:

1. Identify abnormal resource usage.
2. Find the process PID.
3. Determine the parent process.
4. Verify whether it is expected.
5. Gracefully terminate if necessary.

Learning:
Never start with kill -9.
Always identify the owner, parent process, and purpose first.
